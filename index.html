About Pharmalister!


We are Pharmalister, and we are all about making healthcare better, simpler, and more accessible.

Founded in 2021, we are a team of healthcare enthusiasts with over 45 years of combined experience in healthcare.

What Drives Us?


Our mission is simple: we want everyone to have easy access to affordable healthcare, no matter where they are. We are here to break down barriers and bring healthcare into your daily life.


Mission

We are committed to making healthcare accessible, affordable, and seamless for everyone, regardless of their location.

Vision

Envision a world where accessing healthcare is as easy as ordering your favourite treat online. That is the future we are working tirelessly to create.
