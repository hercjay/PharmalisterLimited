Welcome to Pharmalister Limited!

At Pharmalister, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information, with a specific focus on our business models and the integrated healthcare solution, HealthSenta, including entities and agencies listed on the platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Pharmalister may collect personal information, including but not limited to names, contact details, and professional credentials, for entities and agencies listed on the HealthSenta platform.

Health Data: HealthSenta involves the collection of health-related information for personalized healthcare services. This information is treated with the utmost confidentiality.

Payment Information: In the case of transactions, we collect payment details for secure and seamless financial transactions.

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision: Personal and health information is used to deliver our services, ensuring the best experience for entities and agencies listed on HealthSenta.

Communication: We may contact you for updates, announcements, and relevant information related to our services.

Analytics: Data analytics are used to enhance our services and better understand user needs.

Your Choices:

Opt-Out: Individuals, entities and agencies listed on HealthSenta have the right to opt-out of certain data collection and communications.

Access and Correction: You can access and correct your personal information at any time.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Compliance with Regulations

Pharmalister operates in accordance with data protection regulations in Nigeria, the United States, and Europe. Our commitment is to uphold the highest standards of data privacy and security.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties without your consent, except for trusted partners involved in service delivery on HealthSenta.

Children's Privacy

While our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18, guardians and parents are encouraged to guide their wards or children to safely sign up and use the platform under their supervision.
